Mission-driven

Not just climbing — building.

Growing up as a first-generation college student, there was no alumni network to tap, no parent who’d navigated a university application, no blueprint. What Carlos had instead was an instinct to figure it out — which led to the Air Force at 19, a geology degree at Texas A&M, and a decade of operations work managing millions in inventory, reading data, and training teams without a formal management title.

Now enrolled at the Bauer College of Business — studying Data Analytics, Corporate Strategy, and Financial Management — the MBA is the tool to translate that operational foundation into leadership at scale. And to open doors for the next first-generation kid watching from the outside.
